## 🧠 Why Digi Compta?

Every day, Tunisian accounting firms spend hours manually entering invoice data, verifying amounts, and reformatting information for tax declarations. It is repetitive, error-prone, and costly work.

**Digi Compta eliminates this bottleneck.** By combining OCR powered by Mistral AI, ultra-fast inference from Groq, and workflow automation with n8n, the system transforms a raw PDF into structured, usable data within seconds and without human intervention.

---

## 🔍 The Core of the Project: Automated OCR

The centerpiece of Digi Compta is its intelligent OCR pipeline — a system designed to understand and extract data from Tunisian and French-language invoices with high accuracy.

### How It Works

1. Upload a PDF
2. n8n triggers the workflow
3. Mistral AI analyzes the document
4. Groq accelerates inference
5. Data is normalized
6. The invoice editor is automatically populated
7. Results are broadcast in real time

---

## 🤖 The AI Ecosystem: Groq + Mistral + n8n

### Mistral AI — The Extraction Engine
Provides document understanding and structured data extraction.

### Groq — Ultra-Fast Inference
Delivers OCR responses with extremely low latency.

### n8n — Workflow Orchestrator
Coordinates uploads, OCR processing, extraction, validation, and response delivery.
